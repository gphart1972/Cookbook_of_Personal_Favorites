# Harvey Wallbanger Cake

- 1 Box Orange Supreme Cake Mix
- 1 Small box Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
- 4 Eggs
- 1/2 cup oil
- 4 ounces orange juice
- 4 ounces Amaretto
- 1 ounce Vodka

Mix well and pour into prepared bundt pan (I use Baker's Joy).

Bake at 350° for 45 minutes.

Cool in pan 10 minutes.

While cake is still warm apply glaze.

## Glaze

- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 ounce orange juice
- 1 ounce Amaretto
- 1 ounce Vodka

Mix together glaze mixture.

Drizzle over cake or apply with pastry brush.

Enjoy!
