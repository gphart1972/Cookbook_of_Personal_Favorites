# Vegetable Soup with Sausage

A hearty, flavorful vegetable soup featuring sausage, mixed vegetables, and a well-seasoned broth. An excellent variation of Hamburger Soup with a richer, smokier flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1 (quart-size) can diced tomatoes
- 1 can green beans, drained
- 1 can green peas, drained
- 1 can sliced potatoes, drained
- 1 can sliced carrots, drained
- 1 can sweet corn, drained
- 1 large can water (use the empty tomato can as a measure)
- 1½ cups onion, medium diced
- 1½ pounds sausage, sliced
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a large stock pot, combine the diced tomatoes, green beans, green peas, sliced potatoes, sliced carrots, sweet corn, and one large can of water.
2. Place over medium heat and cook for **1 hour**, stirring occasionally.
3. Add the diced onion and sliced sausage.
4. Stir well to combine.
5. Add the salt, garlic powder, and black pepper.
6. Stir thoroughly and increase the heat slightly to bring the soup to a gentle boil.
7. Continue cooking for **1 additional hour**, stirring occasionally.
8. Once the sausage has rendered some of its fat and the broth has developed flavor, reduce the heat to low.
9. Serve hot.

## Notes

- Smoked sausage, kielbasa, or andouille all work well in this recipe.
- Draining the vegetables helps keep the broth clean and lets the sausage flavor shine through.
- Taste before serving and adjust seasonings if needed.
- This soup stores and reheats exceptionally well, making it a great make-ahead meal.

**Yield:** Approximately 8–10 servings
